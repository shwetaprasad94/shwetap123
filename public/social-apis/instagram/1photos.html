Instagram

Photos and Video hosting platform

Photography

Photos will be hosted by Instagram. We will leverage the tools provided by Instagram especially input and image manipulation services. Apps are available for mobile devices for convient photo uploading.

Goal

Our client has used the Instagram iOS app to upload photos of their YVR to Instagram which are available to the public.

As a user, I want to see photos of the YVR

  1. Using jQuery AJAX, connect to Instagram to request photos
  2. Copy and Paste one JPG path, and use jQuery to add one photo to page
  3. Dynamically read the JPG path, and use jQuery to add one photo to page
  4. Loop through Instagram response and display all photos to page

Technologies

  • Instagram API
  • JavaScript
  • jQuery
  • jQuery AJAX
  • node.js
  • hapi.js
